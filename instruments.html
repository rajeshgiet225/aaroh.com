BEHIND THE EAR- BTE

Behind-The-Ear (BTE) hearing aids are used for all types of hearing losses from mild to profound. They come in a variety of styles from Miniature-BTEs to the larger Super Power instruments. Many BTE hearing aids offer multiple directional microphone systems for improved understanding of speech in noisy situations.

 

 Devices available in BTE Technology:

 

  • MOTION MICON
  • AQUARIS
  • NITRO BTE
  • NITRO MICON
  • ORION
  • SIRION
  • INTUIS
  • INTUIS PRO
  • LOTUS PRO

RECEIVER IN CANAL- RIC

Receiver In Canal (RIC) hearing aids are designed for friendly usage under any situations, these are minute in design structure and compatible to fit directly in to the canal, these are made to automatically adjusts itself to your preferences. In addition, nearly all models offer wireless connectivity and onboard controls for convenience and easy manual control. Helps you stay connected and get the most out of life.

 

 

Below is the list of Brand Devices available in RIC Technology:

 

  • BINAX
  • PURE
  • ORION

IN THE EAR - ITE

In-The-Ear (ITE) hearing aids are also known as custom hearing aids, as they are individually manufactured to suit a person's ear. They range from the so-called Micro-CIC instrument, which fits completely in the ear canal to the full-shell hearing instrument, which completely fills the bowl of the ear.

 

Below is the list of Brand Devices available in ITE Technology:

 

  • LOTUS
  • INSIO
  • NITRO CUSTOMS
  • ORION
  • SIRION
  • INTUIS

IN THE CANAL - ITC

In the Canal – (ITC), here the device can sit in the ear canal and can go deeper to the Ear. Fit inside the ear, nearly concealing the hearing instrument, Because hearing instrument is inside ear, the pinna and outer ear provide good directionality via omni-directional microphone, Design options available for these application requirements.

 

 

Below is the list of Brand Devices available in ITC Technology:

 

 

  • LOTUS
  • INTUS
  • ORION
  • SIRION
  • NITRO

COMPLETELY IN CANAL - CIC

Completely In Canal (CIC) is one of the smallest hearing aid models and is especially suitable if you have a mild to moderate hearing loss. It is a custom made shell that contains all the electronic components.

The shell is placed deeply in the ear canal, making the hearing aid almost invisible. A CIC therefore has a big aesthetic advantage: No one can actually see that you are wearing a hearing aid.For easy everyday removal, the CIC has a small extension cord attached to the outside. A CIC model works fully automatically.

 

 

Below is the list of Brand Devices available in CIC Technology:

 

 

  • LOTUS
  • INTUS
  • SIRION
  • ORION
  • NITRO
